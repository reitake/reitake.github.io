Read Me:

  • The script runs entirely on the client side. It will not use any network bandwidth to upload your image or create badges;
  • For mission badges, Niantic recommends using 512x512px images. For a good quality mission set, I'd recommend you use a very large image (at least 2000px wide);
  • Niantic in the Official Mission Criteria says that they will no longer accept mission series larger than 18 missions;

Attention: Due to recent updates of the Chrome browser the tool may fail to generate the largest 6x7 banner. In that case either lower the output resolution or use Firefox.
